03 My Playground

Driven by a desire to constantly learn new technologies, and my passion for a holistic approach to design and development, I have been immersed in the world of web-based 3D programming and front-end development.

Check out the living room and galaxy scenes, both developed using Three.js and Web.GL to create interactive 3D experiences or hover over the hippo for a fun surprise.

I also enjoy creating interactive and wholesome SVG illustrations and practical single page applications. The hippo and lunar animations are both created in Adobe Illustrator before being exported in SVG, added to HTML, and styled and animated with a combination of CSS and Javascript. The game of tenzies, the tip calculator app, or the product launch countdown page were all developed using GIT, HTML, CSS, Javascript and a sprinkle of React.

04My Journey

Over the past ten years, I've built products for companies, businesses and government entities around the globe with a focus on design, usability, accessibility and inclusivity.

While the products I design have been in the built environment, the skills I have learned as an architect and designer are all applicable to the digital world. From working with multi-disciplinary teams, to iterating through design concepts and working with stakeholders, I am committed to meeting client expectations while creating great user experiences.
