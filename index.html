01. About me

I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2015 when I was editing custom Squarespace themes for my architectural portfolio — turns out hacking together a custom contact form taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

Fast-forward to fall 2021, I decided to start the front end developer training on Codecademy and pursue a carreer change in front end developement.

Tell me more!

Well since you've asked, I was born and raised in the south of France 🥖 (yes, I love cheese!). During my bachelors at the University of Clermont-Ferrand, I had the opportunity to study abroad in the US. One thing led to another, and here I am, ten years later, living and working in Washington D.C. But after a decade working as an architect, I have decided to take my technical knowledge and to translate it to the digital world.

In my downtime, you can find me eating tacos, biking, or at the dog park.
